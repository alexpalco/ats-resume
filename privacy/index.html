Privacy Policy

Your privacy is the foundation of ATS Resume Builder.

Last updated: June 2026

ATS Resume Builder stores all resume data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. ATS Resume Builder operates entirely on your local device. There are no user accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

How Your Data Is Stored

Your resumes, job descriptions, ATS scores, and settings are stored in a local SQLite database within the app's sandboxed container on your Mac. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export, email, or back it up.

AI Features

When you use AI-powered features (resume tailoring, ATS scoring with AI, skill gap analysis, resume import), your resume text and the relevant job description are sent to a secure API relay for processing. This relay:

  • Proxies requests to the AI service for processing
  • Does not store, log, or retain any resume content
  • Uses rate limiting by an anonymous device identifier (no personal data)

AI features are optional and require purchasing credit packs. You can build, edit, preview, and export resumes without ever using AI features.

Gmail Integration

If you choose to connect your Gmail account to send resumes directly from the app, ATS Resume Builder requests permission to send emails on your behalf via Google OAuth. We do not read, store, or access any other email data. OAuth credentials are fetched securely from our relay and tokens are stored locally on your device. You can disconnect at any time.

In-App Purchases

Credit packs are purchased through the Mac App Store. Apple handles all payment processing. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

ATS Resume Builder uses the following third-party services only when you explicitly trigger them:

  • AI Processing: resume tailoring, AI review, and import requests are processed via a secure relay
  • Apple App Store: processes in-app purchases
  • Google OAuth: optional Gmail integration for sending resumes

No advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or tracking services are used.

Children's Privacy

ATS Resume Builder does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version here with a new effective date.
