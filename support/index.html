Getting Started

How do I create a resume?

Click "New Resume" on the dashboard, give it a name, and start filling in sections. The live preview on the right updates as you type. When you're ready, export to PDF or Word.

Can I import an existing resume?

Yes. Click "Import Resume" on the dashboard and select a PDF or DOCX file. AI will parse your resume into structured sections (uses 1 credit). You can also create a resume from scratch for free.

ATS Scoring

What is an ATS score?

ATS (Applicant Tracking System) software scans resumes for keywords that match a job description. Your ATS score shows how well your resume matches a specific job posting. A higher score means a better chance of getting past the automated filter.

How do I score my resume against a job?

Open a resume, click "+ Add Job" in the header, and paste a job description. The app will score your resume and provide an AI review with specific feedback and suggested improvements.

AI Credits

How do AI credits work?

AI features like resume tailoring, ATS scoring, skill gap analysis, and resume import each cost 1 credit. Credits are purchased as in-app credit packs through the Mac App Store. You get 3 free credits when you first install the app.

What happens if I run out of credits?

All non-AI features continue to work: resume editing, live preview, PDF/DOCX export, Gmail sending, and backup/restore. Only AI-powered features require credits.

Export & Sharing

How do I export my resume?

Open a resume in the editor and click "Export PDF" or "Export DOCX" in the header. Both formats use a clean, single-column layout with standard fonts — optimized for ATS compatibility.

Can I email my resume directly from the app?

Yes. Click "Send Email" in the editor, connect your Gmail account, and send your resume with the PDF attached. Your Gmail credentials are stored locally and you can disconnect at any time.

Data & Privacy

Where is my data stored?

All your resumes, job descriptions, and scores are stored locally on your Mac in the app's sandboxed container. Nothing is synced to the cloud.

How do I back up my resumes?

Click "Export Backup" in the dashboard footer. This saves all your resumes, job descriptions, and scores as a single JSON file. To restore, click "Restore from Backup" and select the file.
